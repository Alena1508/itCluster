About IT in Ukraine

IT in Ukraine has a long and successful history.

From creating the first computer in Europe in 1951 to becoming our third largest exports branch, after agro and metallurgy, IT was constantly developing and is now one of the most promising industries of Ukraine, with companies of all sizes, working with startups, midsize and Fortune 500 companies, developing ideas and announcing own loud startup acquisitions by international IT giants, having 80% of export service companies, one of the most developed bitcoin communities and so much more.
