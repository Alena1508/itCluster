About KYIV IT Cluster

We are the NGO and community of KYIV IT Companies and related ecosystem.

We unite, educate, strengthen, inspire our members and help them identify their mutual interests and reach their common goals, be it projects with education and interaction with local universities, work with authorities for joint programs or help in establishing good sound partnerships with international partners and clients.
